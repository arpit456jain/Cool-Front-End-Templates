About Us

Arcane art gallery is a New york based Art Gallery which deals in the abstract art by artists all over the World. It was established in 1990 by New York based artist Catharine Salvatore. The name Arcane (meaning abstract) describes the gallery's vision of how abstract art is mysterious yet speaks thousand of words at first glance.
